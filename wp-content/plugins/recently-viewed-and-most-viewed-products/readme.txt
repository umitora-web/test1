== Description ==

"Recently viewed and most viewed products" provides an extra feature to merchant to show 'recently viewed products' and 'most viewed products' on the product detail page and also at any place through out the site by adding given shortcode.

= Main features =

* Merchants can mark/unmark **Recently viewed** and **Most viewed** products manually.
* It allows Merchant to display **Recently viewed** and **Most viewed** products through out their site.
* Merchant can manage number of products to be displayed . 
* Separate **shortcodes** are provided for both recently and most viewed products.
* **Shortcodes can be used anywhere throughout the site to display the products**.
* **posts_per_page and number_of_products_in_row variables parameters can be used with shortcode**.
* There is a feature to display only instock products.
* Hide Recently Viewed & Most Viewed Products on the basis of user roles.
* Seprate settings to hide RV/MV column from woocommerce product list table for selected roles.
* Merchant can view list of **Recently viewed** and **Most viewed** products.
* Separate settings are provided for both recently and most viewed products.
* Separate **enable/disable** feature for both recently and most viewed products.
* Include **External/Affiliate Product** in recently and most viewed list.
* **Theme compatible**
* Simple user interface.
* **Excellent language support**
* Easy to use and install
* **Easy to configure**

= Minimum Requirements =

* WordPress 3.8 or greater
* Woocommerce 2.6.0 or greater
* PHP version 5.2.4 or greater
* MySQL version 5.0 or greater

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Recently viewed and most viewed products, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type "Recently viewed and most viewed products" and click Search Plugins. Once you've found our Recently viewed and most viewed products you can view details about it such as the point release, rating and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by simply clicking "Install Now".

The manual installation method involves downloading our Recently viewed and most viewed products and uploading it to your webserver via your favourite FTP application.
